# Mike's Mom's Potato Salad

**Source:** Family recipe (handwritten scan)  
**Servings:** ~10–12

---

## Ingredients

- 12 medium **red skin potatoes** — cooked and peeled
- 9 **hard boiled eggs** — peeled
- 3 **celery stalks** — thinly sliced
- 1 **small onion** — super fine chop
- 1 tablespoon **dill relish** (or chopped dill pickle)
- 1 cup **Miracle Whip**
- ¼ cup **mustard**
- ¼ cup **milk**
- **Salt and pepper** to taste
- **Paprika** for garnish

---

## Instructions

1. Cook potatoes until tender. Drain, cool, and peel. Slice or cube.
2. Hard boil eggs. Cool, peel, and chop.
3. Thinly slice celery. Finely chop onion.
4. In a bowl, whisk together Miracle Whip, mustard, and milk.
5. Combine potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, and dill relish. Fold in dressing gently.
6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Dust with paprika for garnish.
8. Refrigerate until serving.

---

## Notes

- Hand mixing recommended; Miracle Whip and milk kept separate on the side for adjusting consistency.
- Salt and pepper separate to taste.
- Best made the day before — flavors meld overnight.
