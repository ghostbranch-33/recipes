# Kirkland Signature Spiral Sliced Ham

> Slow-cooked over hickory wood, bone-in, fully cooked. Delicious served cold or warmed.

**Source:** Kirkland Signature label (Costco)  
**Serves:** 12–16  
**Ham weight:** 7–11.5 lbs

---

## Meal Planning Chart

| Method | Bake Time | Temp |
|--------|-----------|------|
| Ham only | 12–15 min/lb (~2 hrs) | 275°F covered |
| Ham + glaze (step 1) | 12–15 min/lb | 275°F covered |
| Ham + glaze (step 2) | 8–10 min | 425°F uncovered |

*USDA recommends reheating precooked hams to an internal temperature of 140°F.*

---

## Warming Without Glaze

1. Heat oven to 275°F.
2. Remove all packaging, including the clear button on the bone.
3. Place ham flat/face side **down** in a shallow roasting pan. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
4. Bake at 275°F until internal temperature reaches 140°F (check after first 90 minutes). **Do not overcook.**
5. Remove from oven; let stand covered for about 15 minutes before serving.

---

## Warming With Glaze

1. Heat oven to 275°F.
2. Remove all packaging, including the clear button on the bone.
3. Place ham flat/face side **down** in a shallow roasting pan. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
4. Bake at 275°F until internal temperature reaches 140°F. **Do not overcook.** Check after first 90 minutes.
5. Remove ham from oven and increase temperature to 425°F.
6. Prepare glaze per the included glaze packet instructions.
7. Brush or spoon glaze over ham and between slices.
8. Bake **uncovered** at 425°F for 8–10 minutes.
9. Remove from oven; let stand 10 minutes before serving.

---

## Storage

- Refrigerate. Once opened, use within 3–5 days.
- Wrap leftovers tightly and freeze up to 3 months.
- If unopened and freezing, place in freezer before the "Use By" date; may keep up to 6 months.
- To thaw: refrigerate 24–36 hours before serving.
